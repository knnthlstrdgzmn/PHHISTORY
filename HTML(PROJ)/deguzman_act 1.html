Business Tips

Tips for Effective Business Planning

  1. Clearly define your business idea and be able to succintly articulate it. Know your mission
  2. Examine your motives. Make sure that you have a passion for owning a business and for this particular business
  3. Be willing to commit to the hours, discipline, continous learning and the frustrations of your owning your business
  4. Conduct a competitive analysis in your market, including products, proces, promotions, advertising, distribution, quality and be aware of the outside influences that affect your business.
  5. Seek help from other small business, vendors, professionals, goverment agencies, employees, trade associations and trade shows. Be alert and ask questions.

Tips on Building a Sound Business Plan

  1. Write a business plan with a complete financial anf marketing plan
  2. Your marketing strategy should be built around your strengths, your competitors's weaknesses and your customer's desires.
  3. Test the reality of your business--know wht it will work and how will make it work. think your business through step by step.
  4. Allow at least two hours every week for thinking and planning. do not allow anything to interfere with this time. you run business. Don't let it run you.
  5. establish an annual operating plan. review it and upgrade it monthly woth appropraite employees
