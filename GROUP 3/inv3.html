Roberto L. del Rosario

is a Filipino entrepreneur who served as the president of the Trebel Music Corporation. He is also the patent holder of the Sing Along System, a karaoke system he developed in 1975.Del
Rosario holds a patent to the Sing Along System, a karaoke machine which he developed in 1975.[1] He filed a patent infringement case against a Chinese company before the Supreme Court. The court
ruled that the company violated his patent rights over his karaoke machine in 1996. He is recognized as the sole holder of a patent for a karaoke system in the world. However most scholars
credits Daisuke Inoue, a Japanese, as the inventor of the karaoke. Inoue invented the karaoke in 1971.
